Hiking Mount Toubkal, the highest peak in North Africa, is an adventure of a lifetime. Here are essential details:

### Best Time to Visit
The best time to hike Mount Toubkal is from late spring (May) to early Autumn (October) when the weather is most favorable.

### What to Bring
- Sturdy hiking boots
- Warm clothing
- A good sleeping bag
- Food and water
- First aid kit

### The Route
The most popular route starts from Imlil, leading to the Toubkal Refuge and then to the summit.
